    About the developers

    The developers of Celeste are Extremly OK Games, Ltd

    They have also made other games like Towerfall and as of recently

    they made the game Celeste 64: Fragments of the Mountain

    How was Celeste made?

    The development of Celeste started all the way back in 2015

    when the game developers Maddy Thorson and Noel Berry participated

    in a game jam. There they created a version of Celeste for the PICO-8.

    They then wanted to expand it into a full game. Being inspired by

    Thorson's previous game Towerfall and Super Mario Bros. 3 they made

    the gameplay minimal and made it mirror the feeling of rock climbing

    They made Celeste to be accessible, featuring an assist mode to make

    the game more forgiving and less challenging. In order to create

    a more "introspective" game, the developers added themes

    of self-forgiveness into the narrative, which grew to become

    intertwined with the gameplay.
